About Me

Hi, My name is Keve Cohen, I am a graduate of Eleven Fifty Academy. I'm also in the process of earning a bachelors of science at Indiana University, majoring in informatics with a concentration in business. I would describe myself as clever, curious, and always ready to learn a new skill. I was born and raised in Indy but I love to travel and explore new places. I started coding in my senior year of high school and decided to pursue tech endeavors in college and out. I've always thought of myself as a logical person and technology also allows me to be creative in my work which I really value.
